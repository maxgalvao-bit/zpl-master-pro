# Privacy Policy

**Last updated:** March 2026

## 1. Who we are
ZPLMaster Pro is a ZPL tools platform for e-commerce,
developed by G-Max Solutions, based in Brazil.

## 2. Data we collect
**We do not collect ZPL label data.**
All ZPL code processing happens locally in your browser —
no label data is transmitted to or stored on our servers.

We only collect:
- **Anonymous usage data** via Google Analytics 4
  (pages visited, session duration, country of origin)
- **Email address** when you voluntarily subscribe
  to the Premium waitlist

## 3. How we use data
- Usage data: to improve our tools and understand 
  which features are most useful
- Email: to notify you when ZPLMaster Premium launches

## 4. Your rights (GDPR)
Under the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR),
EU residents have the right to:
- Access, correct, or delete your personal data
- Object to or restrict processing
- Data portability
- Withdraw consent at any time
- Lodge a complaint with your local supervisory authority

To exercise your rights: suporte@zplmaster.com

## 5. Cookies
We use analytics cookies (Google Analytics) to understand
site usage. You can disable cookies in your browser settings.

## 6. Data sharing
We do not sell or share your data with third parties, except:
- Google Analytics (anonymous usage data)
- Brevo/Sendinblue (email list management)

## 7. Data retention
Waitlist emails are kept until unsubscription or for a
maximum of 2 years after program termination.
